Help: Flappy Bed Fly

Welcome to Flappy Bed Fly! Below are some instructions to help you get started:

Instructions:

  1. Tap or click Enter on the Keyboard to make the bed flap its wings and ascend.
  2. Avoid hitting the walls and obstacles as you fly. Colliding with them will end the game.
  3. Challenge yourself to beat your high score with each attempt.
  4. Share your score with friends and see who can fly the farthest!

If you have any further questions or encounter any issues while playing, feel free to contact us for assistance. Have fun and happy flying!
